Frequently Asked Questions

How to upload documents?

Navigate to the Dashboard, locate the Document Upload section, and simply drag and drop your files into the marked zone. You can also click the area to browse and select files from your computer. Supported formats include PDF, DOC, and DOCX up to 50MB.

How to track my order?

You can track the live status of your requests by clicking on "My Orders" in the sidebar menu. Here, you will see a detailed list showing which orders are pending, processing, or completed.

How to use points?

Reward points can be applied on the "My Points" tab. Once you have enough points, browse the available redemption options (e.g., 5% Discount, Free Printing) and click the respective button to claim the reward. It will automatically be reflected in your wallet or next bill.
